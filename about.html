Built to build

Built by makers, for makers

Onefinity started with a simple idea: a CNC machine should be rigid, reliable and ready to cut out of the box — without the endless tinkering that defined the early hobby market. We designed around precision ball screws and hardened linear shafts so you spend your time making, not maintaining.

Today tens of thousands of Onefinity machines run in home shops, classrooms and production floors across the world — each backed by a North American support team that actually answers the phone.

What we believe

Principles we machine into every unit

Rigidity first

All-metal frames and ball-screw motion for clean, repeatable cuts in tough material.

Truly plug & play

Spindle, laser, rotary and ATC are officially supported add-ons — not community hacks.

Real support

A North American team and a 40k-strong community that genuinely has your back.
